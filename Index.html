Indonesian Halal Compliance Solutions for Indian Industry

Under the Indonesian Halal Product Assurance Law (UU JPH), products entering the market require formal local registration. Navigating the state-run SIHALAL portal (BPJPH) and matching local raw-material matrices requires expert configuration.

⚠️ CRITICAL TIMELINE NOTICE: On October 17, 2026, full enforcement begins for all cosmetics, personal care items, traditional medicines, chemical ingredients, consumer utilities, and Class A medical devices. Products without active BPJPH registration will face customs holds.

Our Specialized Service Frameworks for India

1. SHLN Foreign Certificate Registration (The MRA Framework)
For Indian factories holding active certification from recognized local bodies like Jamiat Ulama-i-Hind Halal Trust, Halal India Pvt. Ltd., or JUHF. We act as your Indonesian Legal Proxy to upload documents to SIHALAL and acquire your official Foreign Halal Registration Number.
2. Direct BPJPH Audit Consulting (Regular Route)
For plants utilizing uncertified facilities. We build your mandatory internal Halal Assurance System (SJPH), map ingredient trace chains, and coordinate logistics with Indonesian inspectors (LPH) visiting your production facility.
3. Agri-Produce Exemption Clearance (Positive List)
For merchants exporting raw agricultural commodities (e.g., pure whole dried chili, raw seeds). We construct specific Product Composition Declarations to clear customs smoothly under the "Halal Positive List" exemption pathways without full certification costs.
