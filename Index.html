Why QC Halal?

In a world of complex trade laws, QC Halal acts as your dedicated compliance proxy. We ensure your documentation meets the strict Indonesian standards for halal product assurance, removing the guesswork from your supply chain.

Services: India Market

Indian exporters face specific scrutiny regarding raw material sourcing and the SJPH (Halal Assurance System). We specialize in:

  • MRA Foreign Certificate Filing: Seamless integration of local Indian certificates into the SIHALAL portal.
  • Ingredient Traceability Audit: Mapping every chemical, additive, and byproduct in your manufacturing process.
  • Customs Clearance Support: Preparing your "Product Composition Declaration" to ensure your shipments are processed without delay.

Note: As of 2026, mandatory certification applies to a vast range of goods. Our team ensures your factory is audit-ready.
