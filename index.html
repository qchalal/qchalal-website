Why QC Halal

QC Halal specializes in bridging the gap between international manufacturers and Indonesia's mandatory Halal regulatory framework. Our expertise helps businesses avoid delays, reduce compliance risks, and secure access to one of the world's largest Muslim consumer markets.


We provide strategic guidance for BPJPH compliance, SIHALAL applications, audit preparation, certification maintenance, and long-term regulatory support.

Services – India

For Indian manufacturers and exporters, entering Indonesia requires strict compliance with the mandatory Jaminan Produk Halal (JPH) framework administered by BPJPH. Certification is now a legal requirement for products circulating in Indonesia.

Target Industries

  • Food & Beverage Manufacturing
  • Pharmaceuticals & Nutraceuticals
  • Cosmetics & Personal Care
  • Hotels, Restaurants & Catering (HORECA)
  • Logistics & Cold Chain Operations

Certification Pathways

MRA Path: Registration of foreign Halal certificates recognized by BPJPH.

Regular Path: Full SIHALAL application, LPH audit, MUI fatwa process, and BPJPH certification.

Services – Indonesia

Entering the Indonesian market requires strict compliance with BPJPH mandatory Halal regulations. We streamline your path to market entry.

  • MRA Certification Path: Registration of recognized foreign Halal certificates.
  • Regular Certification Path: SIHALAL submission, audit guidance, and SJPH implementation.
  • Compliance Maintenance: Renewals, surveillance audits, and ongoing regulatory support.
